Privacy Policy

Glowy Wallpapers  ·  Last updated: April 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Glowy Wallpapers ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using the App you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Device information: Device model, OS version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information used for analytics and ad serving.
  • Usage data: Pages viewed, features used, wallpapers downloaded or favorited, and session duration — collected through Firebase Analytics.
  • Advertising data: Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks) collected by Google AdMob for serving relevant ads.
  • Crash reports: Diagnostic data collected via Firebase Crashlytics to improve app stability.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information unless you explicitly provide it.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App.
  • To display wallpapers and personalise your in-app experience.
  • To serve advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • To analyse usage patterns and fix bugs via Firebase.
  • To send push notifications about new content (only if you grant permission).

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics — analytics and crash reporting
  • Google Play Services

Each service has its own privacy policy; we encourage you to review them.

4. Storage Permissions

The App requests access to your device's photo gallery solely to save wallpapers you choose to download. We do not read, upload, or share any photos already on your device.

5. Push Notifications

We may send push notifications to inform you of new wallpaper collections. You can opt out at any time through your device's notification settings.

6. Data Retention

Usage and analytics data is retained for up to 14 months as per Firebase's default retention policy. Locally cached wallpaper data is stored on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the App.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of personalised advertising via your device's ad settings.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
